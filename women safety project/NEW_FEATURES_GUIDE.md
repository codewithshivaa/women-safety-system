## 2. 📞 Fake Call Feature

### What It Does
Simulates a realistic incoming phone call to help you escape uncomfortable or dangerous situations.

### Features
- **Realistic Call Screen**: Looks like a real incoming call
- **Customizable Caller**: Set any name and number
- **Delayed Calls**: Schedule call after X seconds
- **Ringtone & Vibration**: Full audio and haptic feedback
- **Call Timer**: Shows call duration when "answered"
- **Quick Presets**: Mom, Dad, Friend, Office, Doctor

### How to Use

#### Immediate Call:
1. Go to **Fake Call** tab
2. Set caller name (e.g., "Mom")
3. Set caller number (e.g., "+91 98765 43210")
4. Keep delay at 0
5. Click "Start Fake Call Now"
6. Phone rings immediately
7. Click "Answer" to start fake conversation
8. Use as excuse to leave

#### Scheduled Call:
1. Set caller details
2. Set delay (e.g., 30 seconds)
3. Click "Schedule Call"
4. Continue your activity
5. Phone rings after delay
6. Answer and leave situation

### Use Cases
- Uncomfortable dates or meetings
- Suspicious situations
- Unwanted conversations
- Need excuse to leave quickly
- Feeling unsafe in a location

### Safety Tips
- Keep phone volume up
- Act naturally when "answering"
- Have a prepared excuse ready
- Leave calmly, don't run
- Go to a safe, public place

## 3. 🗺️ Crime Heatmap Visualization

### What It Does
Interactive map showing crime statistics and safety zones in your area.

### Features
- **Visual Crime Data**: Color-coded markers for risk levels
  - 🔴 Red = High Risk
  - 🟡 Yellow = Medium Risk
  - 🟢 Green = Low Risk
- **Crime Type Filters**: View specific crimes
  - ⚠️ Harassment
  - 💰 Theft
  - 🚨 Assault
  - 👁️ Stalking
- **Area Details**: Click markers for detailed info
  - Total incidents
  - Risk level
  - Safety score
  - Description
- **Your Location**: Shows your current position
- **Statistics Dashboard**: Overall crime data
- **Safety Tips**: Location-specific advice

### How to Use

#### View Crime Map:
1. Go to **Crime Map** tab
2. Allow location permission
3. See your location (📍 You)
4. View crime markers around you
5. Click any marker for details

#### Filter by Crime Type:
1. Click filter buttons at top
2. View only selected crime type
3. Click "All Crimes" to reset

#### Check Area Safety:
1. Click on a crime marker
2. View area details panel
3. Check safety score
4. Read incident description
5. Click "View on Google Maps" for directions

### Understanding the Map

#### Marker Colors:
- **Red**: High risk area (10+ incidents)
- **Yellow**: Medium risk (5-9 incidents)
- **Green**: Low risk (1-4 incidents)

#### Safety Score:
- **90-100%**: Safe area
- **60-89%**: Moderate risk
- **0-59%**: Unsafe area

### Safety Recommendations
- **Avoid high-risk areas**, especially at night
- **Stay in well-lit**, populated areas
- **Share location** with trusted contacts
- **Use GPS tracking** in risky zones
- **Report suspicious activity** to police
- **Trust your instincts** - if it feels wrong, leave

## Frequently Asked Questions

A: Yes, it's available 24/7 and responds instantly.

**Q: Does it store my conversations?**
A: No, conversations are only stored in your browser session.

**Q: Can it call emergency services?**
A: No, but it provides emergency numbers and guides you on what to do.

### Fake Call

**Q: Does it actually make a call?**
A: No, it simulates a call screen. No real call is made.

**Q: Can I report crimes?**
