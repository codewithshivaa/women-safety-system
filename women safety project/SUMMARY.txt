🎮 USING THE APP:
──────────────────
1. Open http://localhost:3000
2. Sign up OR Login OR Continue as Guest
3. Add trusted contacts (name + phone number)
4. HOLD the pink SOS button for 1.5 seconds
5. Add optional message
6. Click "Send Alert"
7. Contacts receive SMS with your location + message
8. Check Helplines tab for emergency numbers
9. Use Quick Exit button for privacy
