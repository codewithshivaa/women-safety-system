## 🎯 Common Tasks

### Add Trusted Contact
1. Go to **Contacts** tab
2. Click **+ Add Contact**
3. Enter name and phone (+919876543210)
4. Click **Save**

### Send Emergency Alert
1. Click **SOS Button** (quick click)
2. Allow location permission
3. Alarm sounds + messages sent
4. Click **Stop Alarm** when safe

### Escape Situation
1. Go to **Fake Call** tab
2. Click preset (e.g., "Mom")
3. Click **Start Fake Call Now**
4. Click **Answer** when it rings
5. Pretend to talk, then leave

### Check Area Safety
1. Go to **Crime Map** tab
2. Allow location permission
3. View colored markers
4. Click marker for details
5. Red = avoid, Green = safer

### Ask Safety Question
1. Click **💬 button** (bottom-right)
2. Type question or click quick action
3. Read response
4. Click **✕** to close
